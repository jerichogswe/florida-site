About Tallahassee

Tallahassee is the state capital of Florida. The capital is known for its outdoor activities and aesthetic sceneries.

Facts:

  1. Estimated Population: 202,221
  2. Year Incorporated as a City: 1825
  3. Region of State location: Northern Panhandle
  4. Classification: urban and suburban
  5. Average Income Level: lower (Around $55,000-$60,000)
