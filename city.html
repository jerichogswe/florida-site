About Miami

Miami is the largest city in South Florida and is popularly known for a lot of things, including sandy beaches, stunning views, and nightlife.

Facts:

  1. Estimated Population: 460,289
  2. Year Incorporated as a City: 1896
  3. Region of State location: Southeastern Florida
  4. Classification: urban
  5. Average Income Level: higher (Around $70,000)

About Orlando

Orlando is a city in Florida, popularly known for Disney World as well as Univeral. In addition, there are a ton of attractions, theme parks, and museums

Facts:

  1. Estimated Population: 307,573
  2. Year Incorporated as a City: 1885
  3. Region of State location: Central Florida
  4. Classification: urban and suburban
  5. Average Income Level: higher (Around $65,000)
