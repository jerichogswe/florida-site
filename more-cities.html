About Pensacola

Pensacola is a city in Florida, mainly known for its military history, culture, and beaches.

Facts:

  2. Year Incorporated as a City: 1822
  3. Region of State location: Florida Panhandle

About Tampa

Tampa is a city in Florida, popularly known for its bright districts and mesmorizing waterways. Tampa has a variety of different outdoor activities similar to Tallahassee, including beaches, museums, and attractions.

Facts:

  1. Estimated Population: 408,510
  2. Year Incorporated as a City: 1855
  3. Region of State location: Gulf Coast

  5. Average Income Level: higher (Around $70,000)
